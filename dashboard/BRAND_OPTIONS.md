# Dashboard Brand Options

## Selected for current prototype

**FitScope Global**

- Logo mark: `FS`
- Meaning: a diagnostic scope for global apparel fit, sizing, and review risk.
- Why it fits: professional, product-like, and directly tied to the core insight problem.

## Other viable options

| Name | Logo mark | Fit |
|---|---|---|
| FitBridge | FB | Strong for China-to-overseas positioning; emphasizes bridging fit expectations across markets. |
| SizeSignal | SS | Strong for sizing risk and alerting, but slightly narrower than the full dashboard scope. |
| ApparelScope | AS | Clear and professional; broader than fit, useful if the product expands to assortment and reviews. |
| Hemline IQ | HIQ | More fashion-specific and memorable; slightly more editorial. |
| FitAtlas | FA | Good for global market mapping; more strategic and less operational. |
| ReviewFit Lab | RFL | Academic and research-friendly, but less like a commercial SaaS product. |

## Recommendation

Use **FitScope Global** for the prototype and final demo unless the project shifts away from fit/risk diagnostics.
