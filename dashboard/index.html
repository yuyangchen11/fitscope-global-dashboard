Signals compare transaction demand against article supply. Demand gap = transaction share minus article share.

Scale ↑Transaction share is at least 30% and not meaningfully under-supplied. Treat as a core demand engine.
Potential ↑Demand gap is at least +2.5 percentage points. Demand is stronger than catalog allocation.
Watch →Demand gap is between -2.5 and +2.5 points. Keep monitoring before changing allocation.
Fix ↓Demand gap is at most -2.5 percentage points. Catalog allocation is heavier than transaction response.
